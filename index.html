About

The Albert Games (inspired by Gary Ross' The Hunger Games :) is a short film made by a group NYU freshmen about course regristration! By the end of their 4 years at NYU, they will have gone through more than 8 times regristering for courses!! Classes are generally small so there is a lot of competition when it comes to enrollment. The school's regristration system, Albert, is a key component in the course regristration process. While it does the job, students still find navigating the portal to be a nightmare. This short film, produced in less than 30 minutes by a team of 4 amateur filmmakers, explores the intensity and stress every regristration season!

Movie Title is a short film made by a group NYU freshmen about course regristration! They will go through more than 8 times of course regristration throughout 4 years at NYU. Course regristration is always a nightmare to students because we all are so eager to get the classes we really wanted. The school's regristration system, Albert, is a key factor in the process.
